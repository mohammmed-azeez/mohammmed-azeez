## About Me

I’m Mohammed Azeez, a 22-year-old AI Research Engineer based in Bengaluru (Silicon Valley of India). I build and optimize generative AI models that run on low-resource devices. I’ve led global teams, published research, and fine-tuned LLMs like GPT-4o and Llama 3.1 for Cyber Threat Intelligence. Currently, I’m an AI Research Assistant at the University of Tokyo, focusing on cutting-edge AI strategies for real-world impact.
